Welcome to Know You Studio

At Know You Studio, I believe that everyone has a unique story to tell. My mission is to help you express your individuality through personalized and meaningful products that celebrate who you are. Whether you're looking for custom art, cute accessories, or a special painting for your home, I've got you covered.

Each piece is created with care and attention to detail.

  • Art prints
  • Original paintings
  • Handmade jewelry
  • Custom art and Personalized designs
  • Commissions

Whether you are looking for a premade piece, or need creative direction with bringing your creative vision to life. I am here to help!

Know You!

The name "know you" originated from a joke I had with my friends and family. When they complimented me on my art skills, I would respond with, "NO YOU!" While it started out as a joke, I have come to realize the importance of knowing who I am, expressing myself and encouraging others to do the same. Making art has been a means I've used to learn who I am, explore the world around me and get to know the interworkings of my own mind. I not only hope to share what I make, but to encourage others to do the same.
