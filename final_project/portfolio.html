Portfolio

Below is a small selection of my past work, projects I've worked on and a short description that tells a bit of the story behind them. Enjoy!

The painting shown above represents one of my most significant projects to date. I made this piece during my time serving with an organization called Eurorelief in Lesvos, Greece. Every year hundreds of people make the dangerous trip in a raft from Turkey to the island of Lesvos where they are then held in a camp until they can get their asylum papers and passport registration processed. The months I spent in the camp were some of the most impactful experiences of my life. I love to make work that brings awareness to important issues and the needs of others. If you are interesting in donating or learning more about how you can contribute to supporting the refugee crisis you can check out their website below. Eurorelief

The bus pictured above was also a project I worked on in the camp. Even making small additions of color and beauty to the prison-like camp was a way to spread hope.

This painting was made using fabric scraps I collected from around the sewing machines where the women would work tirelessly to patch up the only clothes they had brought with them from their country.
